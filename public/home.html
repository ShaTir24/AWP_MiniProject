Welcome to our website, dedicated to gathering valuable information about your internship or project experience. We are excited to hear about your achievements, challenges, and the skills you have acquired throughout your journey. Your insights will help us improve the internship and project programs, making them more valuable for future students. Thank you for taking the time to share your experiences with us.

Instructions

  • To access the home page, click on the "Home" link in the navbar. This will take you to the homepage of the website, where you can find some instructions and information about the website.
  • To access the internship page, click on the "Internship" link in the navbar. This will take you to a page where you can enter details of the internship you have done.
  • On the project page, you will find a form where you can enter details about the project you have done. Fill in the required fields and submit the form.
  • On the Kaizen page, you will find a form where you can enter details about your participation in Kaizen activities. Fill in the required fields and submit the form.
